## Contributing

Thank you for your interest in improving this project.

I would greatly appreciate your help with testing and debugging.
If you find a bug, please open an Issue and describe:

* What happened
* What you expected to happen
* Steps to reproduce the problem
* Your operating system and version

If you would like to fix the issue yourself, feel free to submit a Pull Request.

Your help with finding and fixing bugs is highly appreciated — it makes the project better for everyone.
